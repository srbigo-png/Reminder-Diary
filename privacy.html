Personal Secretary

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026

The short version: Personal Secretary keeps your information on your own device. The app has no account, no server of its own, and does not collect, track, or sell your data. Your reminders never leave your phone.

This Privacy Policy explains how the Personal Secretary application ("the app", "we", "us") handles information. It applies to the Personal Secretary app published on Google Play. By using the app, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information we collect

None on our side

Personal Secretary does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers. We do not have servers that receive your data. We do not ask you to create an account, sign in, or provide an email address, name, phone number, or any other identifying detail to use the app.

Everything you create in the app — reminders, titles, notes, times, and lists (such as shopping, health, or family lists) — is stored locally on your device only, inside the app's private storage. We cannot see this information, and it is not uploaded anywhere by the app.

2. Permissions and why the app needs them

The app requests certain device permissions strictly to deliver reminders reliably. Each one is used only for its stated purpose, on your device:

  • Notifications — to show reminder alerts.
  • Alarms & exact timing — to make sure a reminder fires at the exact time you set, even when the device is idle.
  • Full-screen alert — to show an important reminder on screen, like an alarm clock, so it is not missed.
  • Run after restart — so your scheduled reminders still work after the phone is turned off and on.
  • Keep the device awake briefly — only long enough to play the reminder and read it aloud.
  • Microphone — used only for continuous voice dictation in lists, and only while you are actively dictating (see section 3).
  • Bluetooth — used only to detect when your phone connects to a car (car audio), so reminders can wait while you are driving and fire when you leave the car. It is never used to track location or identify devices, and sends no data to us.

None of these permissions are used to gather information about you, and none send data to us.

3. Voice input (microphone)

Handled by Google

The app lets you enter text by voice instead of typing, in two places. In both cases, the speech recognition is performed entirely by the built-in speech recognition engine of Android / Google on your device — never by the app itself.

  • Dictating a single field (a name, title, or note) uses the system's standard "speak now" dialog. This does not require a microphone permission from the app.
  • Continuous dictation in lists (shopping, health, family) uses a microphone permission (RECORD_AUDIO) only while you are actively dictating. The microphone is active only during dictation and turns off automatically when you finish or after a short pause. If you do not grant this permission, the app falls back to the standard "speak now" dialog, which needs no permission.

In all cases, the app does not record, save, or write any audio file. When you speak, the audio is processed by Google's speech engine and only the resulting text is returned to the app and stored locally on your device. Any further processing of that text (such as splitting it into list items or recognizing product names) is done locally on your device.

What happens to the audio inside Google's speech engine — including whether it is processed on the device or on Google's servers — is determined by that engine and by your device settings, and is governed by Google's own Privacy Policy, not by us. You can read it at policies.google.com/privacy.

If you prefer not to use voice input at all, you can simply type your text instead — voice is optional.

4. Text-to-speech (reading reminders aloud)

The app can read a reminder aloud using your device's built-in text-to-speech engine. This happens on your device and does not send your reminder content to us.

5. How we use information

Because the app does not collect your information, we do not use it for any purpose. We do not profile you, show personalized ads, or share information with advertisers or other third parties.

6. Sharing of information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information, because we do not have it. The only third party involved is Google's on-device speech recognition service described in section 3, and only when you choose to use voice input.

7. Children's privacy

The app is a general-purpose reminder tool and is not directed at children. Since the app does not collect any personal information from anyone, it does not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Data retention and deletion

Your reminders and lists remain on your device until you delete them within the app, or until you uninstall the app. Uninstalling the app removes its locally stored data from your device. Since we hold none of your data, there is nothing for us to delete on our side.

9. Security

Your information stays within the app's private storage on your device and is protected by your device's own security (such as your screen lock). Because nothing is transmitted to us, there is no transmission for us to secure.

10. Changes to this policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be reflected here before they take effect.
